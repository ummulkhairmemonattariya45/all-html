BIRYANI

INGREDIENTS:
  • Chicken1/2 kg
  • Onions3 or 4
  • Potatoes (optional)10
  • Tomato1
  • Oil2cup
  • Ginger & Garlic paste1 tps
  • Red chili powder1tps
  • Tumeric1/2 tps
  • Yogurt1/2 cup
  • Coriander1 bunch

METHOD:

  1. Instructions:

    2. Marine the chicken : In a bowl, mix the chicken pieces with yogurt , ginger & garlic paste, chili powder , tumeric powder , cumin powder , and salit . Let it marinate for atleast 1 hour , perfectly longer for better flavor

  2. Cook The Rice :

    3. In a large pot,bring water boil . Add the sooked and drained rice along with whole spices ( cardamom , cloves ,cinnamon) and salt .Cook until tthe rice is 70-80% done. Drain the rice and set asides.

  3. Fry the Onions :

    4. In aseperate pan , heat ghee or oil .Fry the thinly sliced onions until golen brown and crispy . Remove from the oil and set asides

  4. Layering the Biryani :

    5. In a heavy-bottomed pot, spread a layer of marinated chicken at the bottom . Top it with a layer of cooked rice. Sprinkle some chopped tomatoes, green chilies, coriander leaves , mint leaves , and fried onions . Repeat the layers until all the chicken and rice are used up . Finish with a layer of rice on top.

  5. Cooking the Biryani :

    6. Cover the pot tighly with a lid or aluminum foil. Cook on low heat for about 20-25 minutes , or until the chicken is cooked through and the flavours have melded together .You can also place a tawa (geiddle) underneath the pot to prevent buring
